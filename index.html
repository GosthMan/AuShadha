Aushadha

AuShadha (औषध): Means medicine in Sanskrit. This is A Public Health Management System with Django back end and Dojo front end optimised for Indian Primary Health Setup but extensible and/or applicable elsewhere. AuShadha is GPLv3 Licensed.

Aushadha

AuShadha (औषध): Means medicine in Sanskrit. This is A Public Health Management System with Django back end and Dojo front end optimised for Indian Primary Health Setup but extensible and/or applicable elsewhere. AuShadha is GPLv3 Licensed.

Welcome to AuShadha

Welcome to the Home Page of AuShadha project. This is a initiative by me, a Paediatric Orthopaedic and Spine Surgeon practising at Coimbatore, India to develop and Open Source Initiative into Public Health Management.
